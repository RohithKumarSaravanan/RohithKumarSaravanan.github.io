About Me

Hi! I'm Rohith Kumar, a passionate visual storyteller based in Fredericton, NB. My journey into photography began with a fascination for light, shadow, and the infinite stories waiting to be told in everyday moments.

I specialize in landscape, portrait, and event photography, striving to capture authentic emotions and perspectives with vibrant color and clarity. Let's create something beautiful together.
